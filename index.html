Many have claimed that 'The laptop is the new folk guitar'; if this is so, then PB_UP is the first acoustic computer music folk band: The laptop is their only instrument. It was synthesizer, realtime sound processor, networking device, and now it is understood as a fully autonomous musical instrument, including being its own hand-held sound system:

player + laptop = improviser + instrument = music.
> A musical practice of the future emerges, which no longer waits for the development of new types of musical instruments, but takes over instruments that were not specially built for music. And it is precisely because of their limited, ideologically restricted musical use that they become relevant to music - as well as records, tape recorders, radios and other devices of apparently only technical value, which only reproduce sounds. Edgard Varèse - Rückblick auf die Zukunft (Musik-Konzepte 6), Heinz-Klaus Metzger & Rainer Riehn (1983)
